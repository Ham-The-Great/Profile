App info & tips

This app stores everything in your browser (localStorage). Clearing your browser data will remove your products and carts.

Product information is fetched via the community-driven Open Food Facts API when possible, based on the barcode. Supermarket prices are looked up via their public websites, but due to technical limits (like CORS) some price lookups may fail.

When in-store, scan the shelf label or barcode, update the price manually if needed, then open the corresponding store cart to follow your best prices.
